Who am I?

I started making websites in '96.

Found Umbraco in 2007, got involved, etc... voted MVP x5 by the community.

Started an agency, Umbrella, in 2012, we only do Umbraco, main client is Carlsberg.

What is Umbraco?

"The Friendly CMS"

~300,000 active installs

Average 1,000 installs/day

Umbraco is an open-source ASP.NET CMS

Started in 2003; v2 open-sourced in 2005.

It's been around for 10 years, that's a long time in web-tech.

Who uses Umbraco?

Microsoft (ASP.NET, Azure websites)

Tesco, Red Bull, Jaguar, Condé Nast (Wired, Vogue), NFL, ITN, Costa, Heniz, Peugeot, Fox, Warner Brothers, SanDisk, ABBA, Take That...

Why do I use Umbraco?

Simple & Intuitive

Flexible

Extensible

OK, that sounds like marketing bullshit.

It lets you manage your website, and stays out of your design/markup.

APIs & extension points, keeps developer happy.

Focus on content creation & publishing

You don't *need* to know .NET

but if you do, it's super powerful!
